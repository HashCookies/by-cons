Who we are

Founded in July, 2017, Bayraktar Consultants provides professional services, consultancy, and business development for its clients. Professional services include support during the tender proposal process with the goal of successful bids, identifying key growth areas and business strategies, acting as liaison between companies and the client, and helping companies navigate governmental and private entity requirements in the Middle East.

Moving forward, Bayraktar Consultants intends to focus on the infrastructure, information technologies, and food and beverage sectors, as these are the sectors experiencing the most explosive growths around the world.

In July, 2017, Ezra Bayraktar founded Bayraktar Consultants to take advantage of the tremendous growth opportunities in economic success in the Middle East. Educated in the US, Ezra Bayraktar has set-up, operated, and managed business’ for others and decided, like all persons of vision and drive, to make use of that business experience to recently form Bayraktar Consultants.

Bayraktar Consultants value is offering its expertise in three primary areas:

  1. An extensive professional network from Europe to the USA
  2. An ability to connect sub-contractors for different projects in completely different sectors
  3. An ability to match the right people and sub-contractors to the specific projectrequirements, across completely different sectors. The other important issue of expertise of Bayraktar Consultants is to reliably and timely share its expertise with its customers.
